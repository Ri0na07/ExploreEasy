About ExploreEasy

Who We Are

ExploreEasy Travel and Tours & Art Collections was founded in February 2025, located in Looc, Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental. We are an existing business dedicated to making travel planning simple, enjoyable, and affordable.

Our goal is simple: turn your travel dreams into real, unforgettable experiences. Whether you want to explore historical sites, relax on white sand beaches, or discover local traditions — we make it happen.

What We Offer

  • ✅ Flight and hotel reservations
  • ✅ Guided tours and local transportation
  • ✅ Customized travel itineraries
  • ✅ Handcrafted art collections
  • ✅ Special discounts and group rates
